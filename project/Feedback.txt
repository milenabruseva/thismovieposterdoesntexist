Topic 1:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
This Movie Poster Doesn’t Exist                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
Use a (conditional) GAN to generate movie posters. The group cites a paper that uses StyleGan for movie poster generation, but they aim to generate better results with a conditional GAN                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
Feasibility:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
This is a very ambitious proposal, since training times for the GAN will be very long, and if the group wants to not only implement one version, but also improve and extend the architecture, they would have to train many times. But implementing and training an existing architecture would already make for an interesting project. (Note to the group: conditional extensions of StyleGAN already exist, such as https://arxiv.org/pdf/1909.09974.pdf )
